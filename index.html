Guide-AI Workshop @ VLDB2025

Guide-AI seeks to foster convergence between data management researchers and the broad Responsible AI researchers from neighboring communities like AI, Machine Learning, and Theoretical Computer Science. The workshop will publish both regular research papers (8 pages plus unlimited references) and short papers (up to 4 pages) addressing challenges, visionary ideas, and tools related to Responsible AI.

Learn More

Topics of Interest

  • Fairness, accountability, transparency, trustworthiness of all aspects of data management and Machine Learning.
  • Explainability of complex data manipulations.
  • Role of Data Integration and Quality Management in ML pipelines.
  • Database Repair techniques and data manipulations to handle noise and ensure fairness for removing bias.
  • Provenance for responsible data tracking, access control, and controlling data leakage.
  • Use of Causal Inference principles for responsible analytics and explanations.
  • Privacy for responsible data access.
  • Human in the loop tools for responsible AI.
  • Information visualization and UX design and its influence on bias.
  • Software audits and testing, Debugging.
  • System design and Algorithmic impacts on social phenomena.
  • Data Discovery and Governance of data sharing.
  • Interdisciplinary techniques: Collaboration with domain experts.

Important Dates

  • Notification of acceptance: June 10, 2025
  • Camera-ready copy due: July 1, 2025
